How To Make a Pizza

Pizza is one of those foods that taste amazing no matter where you go. Whether it is Italy or America, the crust, marinara sauce, and cheese combo never fails to disappoint. You also have the freedom of choice with this dish

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Pepperoni Slices
  • Sliced Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Beat and twirl the pizza dough until it is in a circular shape
  2. With a ladle, grab a couple scoops of marinara sauce and spread it around the dough
  3. Put the mozzarella cheese over the sauce and add as much or as little as you would like
  4. Add the mushroom and pepperoni to the top of the pizza
  5. Put the pizza on a pizza pan and leave it in the oven at 500 degrees fahrenheit for 10 - 20 minutes
    • The amount of time you leave the pizza in the oven will depend on how thick the crust is and how crispy you would like your crust.
  6. Take the pizza out the oven and leave it to cool
  7. Enjoy!
