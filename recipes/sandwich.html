How To Make a Sandwich

A classic meal that almost anyone can make. You can also customize it as much as you want. The limit of what can fit in between those two slices of bread is infinite. You can add and take away what ever you want. This is the most basic recipe for a sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Bread(of your choice)
  • Ham(or any other type of protein you would prefer)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese you can choose from:
    • Swiss
    • Cheddar
    • American
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Get the two slices of bread and spread the Mayonnaise over both slices of bread.
  2. Wash you lettuce and get one to two pieces and put those pieces over one of the slices of bread
  3. Get two to three slices of ham and put them over the lettuce
  4. Get another one to two pieces of lettuce and put it over the ham
  5. Slice the tomato into two to three slices and put it on top of the lettuce
  6. Put as much mustard as you like on top of the tomatoes
  7. Put the other piece of bread on your creation
  8. Enjoy!

Remember, you can add and take away as much as you want. The possibilities are limitless!
